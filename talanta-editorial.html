02 — The TALANTA Formula

Three moves, one direction

01
Assess

Evidence before opinion

A fact-based understanding of the current state — operations, governance, financial reality — grounded in what we can prove, not what we are told. The assessment is often uncomfortable. It is always useful.

02
Align

Strategy meets structure

Connecting strategy, people, governance, and execution into one coherent direction — so decisions made in the boardroom mean the same thing on the ground floor.

03
Achieve

Results that hold

Measurable, sustainable outcomes — with the measurement discipline and operating rhythm in place so the results outlast our involvement.
