Who We Are

Advisory built on balance

TALANTA is a Riyadh-based advisory and consulting firm. We work alongside executive leadership to assess with evidence, align strategy with execution, and achieve results that outlast the engagement.

The Name

A balance, before it was a talent

TALANTA descends from the Greek talanton — which did not originally mean talent. It meant a balance, and the weight it measures. Only later did it come to mean entrusted value, and later still, ability itself.

That lineage is our operating philosophy: sound judgment begins with right measure. We weigh the evidence before the opinion, balance ambition against capability, and hold strategy and execution on one level beam.

In our region the idea needs no translation — الميزان (the balance) stands for fairness, right measure, and considered judgment. It is the standard we hold our own advice to.
