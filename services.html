What We Do

Services built for executive leadership

Four connected practices, one engagement model. Every service runs on the same formula — assess with evidence, align strategy with execution, achieve results that hold.

01 — Advisory

Trusted counsel at the decision point

Confidential, senior-level counsel to boards, CEOs, and owners on the decisions that shape the organization — strategy, priorities, succession, and the calls that cannot be delegated.

Typical engagement

  • Standing counsel to a CEO or chairman
  • Strategic options appraisal for a major decision
  • Pre-transaction readiness assessment

You walk away with

  • A written, defensible point of view
  • Decision framing the board can act on
  • An advisor who stays reachable
02 — Transformation

From current state to target state

Structured programs that move organizations to where their strategy says they should be — with the pace the Kingdom’s transformation agenda now demands of every serious enterprise.

Typical engagement

  • Enterprise transformation design and delivery
  • Transformation office setup and operation
  • Rescue of a stalled program

You walk away with

  • A sequenced roadmap tied to value, not activity
  • A delivery engine your own people run
  • Milestones the board can hold to account
03 — Governance

Decisions with owners, speed with control

Decision structures and accountability for boards, executive committees, and family councils — increasingly consequential as Saudi family groups formalize under the Companies Law and prepare generational transitions.

Typical engagement

  • Board and committee architecture
  • Family council and continuity design
  • Decision-rights mapping across the group

You walk away with

  • Charters people actually follow
  • Clarity on who decides what, and how fast
  • A governance rhythm that accelerates, not polices
04 — Performance Excellence

Measurement that changes behavior

KPI architectures, operating rhythms, and review cadences that make performance visible and improvement continuous — so results survive leadership changes and market cycles.

Typical engagement

  • Enterprise KPI architecture design
  • Executive review cadence and dashboarding
  • Performance turnaround for a unit or function

You walk away with

  • Metrics the board actually needs to see
  • An operating rhythm that runs without us
  • Early-warning signals, not post-mortems
