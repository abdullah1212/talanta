# TALANTA logo system

**The mark — "the Balance A."** TALANTA descends from the Greek *talanton*: a balance, and
the weight it measures (the root of "talent"). The mark is the brand's A with its crossbar
extended into a level balance beam — one shape carrying three readings:

1. **A** — the letter that carries the brand (Assess, Align, Achieve)
2. **A balance** — *talanton*; also resonant with *mizan* (ميزان) in the Saudi market: right measure, fairness, sound judgment
3. **A summit with a horizon** — ambition held level by alignment ("Aligning Ambition with Achievement")

## Rules

- **Clear space:** 2× the beam overhang (the beam extends one stroke-width past each leg; keep twice that clear on all sides).
- **Minimum sizes:** mark 24px; horizontal lockup 140px wide. Below that, use the mark alone.
- **The beam stays level.** Never rotate, italicize, or skew the mark — a tilted balance contradicts the idea.
- **Gold discipline:** bright gold `#C8A95B` on navy only; ink gold `#826C30` for gold elements on white/light (contrast-safe).
- **Wordmark:** Montserrat ExtraBold, tracking ≈ 0.32em, the three A's in gold. Lockup SVGs use live `<text>` — outline the type (Figma/Illustrator) before sending to print or anywhere Montserrat isn't loaded.
